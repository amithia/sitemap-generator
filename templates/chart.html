site map

Edit mode: drag a card onto another card to move it (with all its sub-pages) under that page; drag onto a SIBLING’s edge (left/right for sections, top/bottom for pages in a list) to reorder without re-parenting; drop on the Standalone panel to detach. Moves never delete anything — a card always carries its whole subtree. “+ New page” adds a blue “planned” card you can drag anywhere. Your draft is a copy — the original map is untouched and comes back when you leave edit mode.

    Standalone pages

    Top-level pages with nothing beneath them — kept out of the map for clarity.

      Branch counts include every page beneath a card. Long lists are folded behind “more” buttons; card titles link to the live pages. Scroll right past the last section for standalone pages. Click a page's link count to see every link found on it.
